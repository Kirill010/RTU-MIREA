IT security is a critical aspect of protecting our computer systems from cybercrimes. 
There are several ways to safeguard our data, and one of the most basic methods is setting strong passwords. 
By using complex and unique passwords for each account and regularly changing them, we can significantly reduce the risk of unauthorized access.
Biometric authentication is another advanced method to protect computer systems. 
This technology uses unique biological characteristics such as fingerprints, iris scans, 
or facial recognition to verify a user's identity, making it extremely difficult for unauthorized users to gain access.
Utilizing antivirus software is essential for detecting and removing malicious software, such as viruses, worms, and trojans that could compromise the security of a computer system. 
Regularly updating antivirus programs and running system scans can help prevent cyber-attacks and data breaches.
A firewall acts as a barrier between your computer and the internet, monitoring incoming and outgoing network traffic to block potentially harmful data packets.
